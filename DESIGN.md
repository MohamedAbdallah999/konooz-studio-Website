# Konooz visual direction

Ink `#171511`, Espresso `#2A2118`, Antique Gold `#B8963E`, Champagne `#D4B968`, Warm Ivory `#F7F1E6`, and Porcelain `#FFFDF8`. Cormorant Garamond carries the fashion-editorial voice; Manrope keeps operational data quick to scan. Both are SIL OFL and bundled in the build.

The layout is an asymmetric atelier ledger: dark sculptural navigation around calm ivory work surfaces. Its signature is a physically lit, breathing champagne-silk mesh. It reduces rendering density on smaller devices and becomes static when reduced motion is requested. Motion is reserved for entry and confirmation; search, checkout, and inventory remain calm.

The system avoids generic purple gradients, floating glass cards, icon-feature matrices, and excessive rounded containers.
